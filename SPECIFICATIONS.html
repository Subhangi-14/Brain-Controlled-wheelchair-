Project Overview

This revolutionary brain-controlled wheelchair system uses electroencephalography (EEG) sensors to interpret brain signals and convert them into wheelchair movement commands. An IR sensor is incorporated for movement by blink detection, ensuring safe navigation in real-world environments. Additionally, a gyroscope helps track head or body orientation to assist in determining direction and stability, enabling more precise control during movement.The system is designed to provide mobility independence for individuals with severe motor impairments who cannot operate traditional wheelchair controls.

Together, these components create a powerful assistive device that offers freedom and independence through a fusion of neuroscience and smart technology.
