Frequently Asked Questions

How does the brain-control interface work?

+

Our brain-controlled wheelchair uses non-invasive electroencephalography (EEG) sensors that detect electrical activity in your brain. The advanced neural network algorithms interpret these signals into movement commands. After a brief training period (typically 1-2 hours), the system learns to recognize your unique thought patterns associated with directional movement, allowing you to navigate simply by thinking about where you want to go.

Is training required to use the NeuroWheel?

+

Yes, a brief training period is necessary to calibrate the system to your unique brain patterns. Our included software guides you through a series of exercises that help the wheelchair learn to interpret your thoughts. Most users achieve basic proficiency within 2 hours, with expert control developing over the first week of use. We provide remote training support with certified technicians to help you through this process.

What if I can't concentrate or get distracted?

+

The NeuroWheel is designed with safety as the top priority. The system requires specific, intentional thought patterns for movement, which helps prevent accidental commands. Additionally, all models include an automatic stop feature that activates when conflicting signals are detected. The Premium and Ultimate models include advanced filters that can distinguish between intentional commands and background thoughts.

Is the NeuroWheel covered by insurance?

+

Many insurance providers and Medicare/Medicaid programs cover the NeuroWheel as an assistive device. Coverage varies based on your specific plan and medical necessity documentation. Our dedicated insurance specialists can help you navigate the approval process, including gathering necessary documentation from your healthcare providers. We also offer financing options and can help connect you with relevant non-profit organizations that provide grants for assistive technology.

What maintenance is required?

+

The NeuroWheel requires minimal maintenance. We recommend charging the battery daily, cleaning the EEG sensors weekly with the provided solution, and scheduling an annual check-up with our certified technicians. The software automatically updates when connected to Wi-Fi. All models include 24/7 remote diagnostics that can detect and often resolve issues before they affect performance.

What is the range and battery life?

+

Battery life varies by model: Standard (10 hours/20 miles), Premium (15 hours/30 miles), and Ultimate (24 hours/45 miles). All models use quick-charge lithium-ion batteries that can reach 80% capacity in under 2 hours. The Ultimate model includes our proprietary energy recovery system that extends range by recapturing energy during deceleration and downhill movement.
